## Tips that move reply rates from 2% to 15%

1. **Send Tue–Thu, 9–11 AM in the recipient's timezone.** Mondays drown, Fridays get ignored.
2. **First name only** in greetings, never "Dear Sir/Madam".
3. **One concrete ask per email.** "Reply yes/no" beats "let me know what you think".
4. **Plain text > HTML.** Looks personal, not marketing.
5. **Follow up once after 5 days.** Two-thirds of replies come on the follow-up.
6. **Track in a spreadsheet**: name, email, date sent, reply, link earned.

Aim to send 5–10 personalized emails per week, not 100 templated. Quality reply rate matters more than volume.
