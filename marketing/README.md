# Phase 3 — Backlink Building Kit

This folder contains everything you need to execute Phase 3 of the SEO growth plan: disavow harmful links, launch on Product Hunt, and systematically build new high-quality backlinks.

## Files

| File | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **`disavow.txt`** | Upload to Google Search Console > Disavow Tool. Tells Google to ignore the 9 spam domains currently linking to you. |
| **`product-hunt-kit.md`** | Full launch kit — copy, taglines, asset checklist, timing, follow-up. |
| **`backlink-targets.md`** | 126 prioritized submission targets across 9 tiers. |
| **`outreach-templates.md`** | 6 cold-email templates for guest posts, directory submissions, link reclamation. |

## Execution order (week-by-week)

### Week 1 — Damage control + foundation
1. **Day 1**: Upload `disavow.txt` to [Google Disavow Tool](https://search.google.com/search-console/disavow-links-tool)
2. **Day 2–3**: Submit to 10 Tier-2 directories from `backlink-targets.md` (AlternativeTo, SaaSHub, ToolFinder, etc.)
3. **Day 4–7**: Prepare Product Hunt assets per `product-hunt-kit.md`

### Week 2 — Launch
1. **Tuesday 12:01 AM PST**: Launch on Product Hunt
2. Same day: Post on Hacker News (Show HN), Indie Hackers, BetaList queue
3. Same week: Submit to remaining Tier-1 platforms

### Week 3–4 — Tier 3 + Reddit warm-up
1. Submit PRs to 5 relevant `awesome-*` repos
2. Start commenting helpfully in r/webdev, r/SideProject (build karma)
3. List on DevHunt, StackShare, OpenAlternative

### Month 2–3 — Outreach
1. Send 5–10 personalized guest-post pitches per week using `outreach-templates.md`
2. Reddit posts (after karma built) in r/SideProject + r/InternetIsBeautiful
3. Fill out HARO / Qwoted profile, answer 2–3 queries per week

### Month 4–6 — Compound
1. Continue 5 outreach emails per week
2. Cross-post each blog article to dev.to + Hashnode (with canonical link to your site)
3. Re-check disavow file monthly — add any new spam in Search Console > Links

## How to measure progress

In **Google Search Console** every Monday, track:
- Total **referring domains** (should grow week-over-week)
- **Top linking sites** (quality should improve)
- **Average position** in Search results (should drop from 74 → 50 → 30 → 10)

In **Semrush** (or the Lovable Semrush built-in tools) monthly:
- **Authority Score** (target: 2 → 15+ in 6 months)
- **Organic keywords** (target: 10 → 200+ in 6 months)

## Expected outcome at 6 months

If you execute consistently:
- **30–60 new quality backlinks** (vs current 13 nofollow spam)
- **Authority Score 15–20** (vs current 2)
- **3,000–8,000 monthly organic visits** (vs current ~5)
- **15–30 keywords ranked on page 1** of Google
