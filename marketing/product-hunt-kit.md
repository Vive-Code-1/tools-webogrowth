## 4. Hunter & supporters strategy

- **Self-hunt** unless you have a top-50 hunter willing to post. A maker post with a strong first comment outperforms a hunter post with a weak one.
- **Pre-launch list** (build 1 week before):
  - Email signup form on tools.webogrowth.com (use Resend connector — already linked)
  - Twitter teaser thread: "Launching X on Product Hunt next Tuesday — here's a 30-second preview"
  - LinkedIn post in your network
  - Bangladesh dev FB groups (BFD, Bangladesh PHP Group, Dhaka Front-end Devs)
  - Discord: Frontend Devs, Indie Hackers, Reactiflux

- **Launch day timing**:
  - 12:01 AM PST → notify pre-launch list immediately
  - 6:00 AM PST → post in Indie Hackers, r/SideProject
  - 9:00 AM PST → engage every comment within 15 min
  - 6:00 PM PST → final push to network

- **Goal**: Top 5 of the day = ~300+ upvotes typically. Top 10 = ~150+.

## 5. Post-launch follow-up (first 72 hours)

- Thank every upvoter who left a comment
- Tweet the badge: "Featured on Product Hunt today 🎉"
- Email pre-launch list: "We made it to #X of the day, thank you"
- Add **"As featured on Product Hunt"** badge to the site footer (proven trust signal)

## 6. Long-tail benefits

PH backlinks are **dofollow** and the producthunt.com domain has Authority Score 93. A single PH listing gives you:
- 1 strong dofollow backlink
- Traffic spike of 2,000–10,000 first-day visitors
- Secondary mentions on aggregators (BetaList, Indie Hackers, Hacker News occasionally)
- Long-tail Google rankings for "your-product-name product hunt"

Even an "okay" PH launch (rank 15–30) is one of the highest-ROI single backlinks you can earn.
